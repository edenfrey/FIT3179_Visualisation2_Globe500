Globe500:
The Top 500 Richest People.

A Data Visualisation by Amir Adrian Amir Hamzah.

Around the world we will find plenty of people. Not all of them are rich, some are. This visualisation explores data about the top 500 richest people and the industry they are associated with retrieved from Bloomberg's Billionaires Index. This data represents the data as of October 13th 2022. Do explore the various visualisations to gain information. For an updated dataset feel free to check out Bloomberg's website.
